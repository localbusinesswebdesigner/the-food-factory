About The Food Factory

Crafting exceptional culinary experiences since 2010

The Food Factory represents the pinnacle of catering excellence in Midrand and beyond. Founded by executive chef Alex Johnson, our company brings together world-class culinary expertise with impeccable service standards.

We specialize in creating bespoke dining experiences for corporate events, weddings, and private gatherings. Our philosophy centers on using only the finest seasonal ingredients, sourced from local producers whenever possible.

With a team of 25 dedicated professionals, we combine innovation with tradition to deliver events that exceed expectations and create lasting memories for you and your guests.
